Lets understand how to interpret each transactions 

| title  | type | means |
|--------|------|-------|
| `MPAY/UPI/TRTR` | UPI transfer | P2P, P2M UPI Transfer |
| `POS/PRCR` | Card payment | Online payment, card swipe, card taps|
| `UPIMANDBTS` | Autopay | Activate AutoPAY with UPI |
| `UPI/LITE/TRTR` | Added to UPI-Lite | Transfer money from Bank to UPI-lite account |
| `RUPAYDECL` | RuPay Card payment | POS/Online payment through RuPay network | 
| `<acount_number>:Int:Pd`| Interest you get|  Interest given to your balance| 
| `CWRD` | ATM cash withdrawal | withdraw cash from ATM |
| `AMC ATMCRD` | Annual ATM card Maintainence charge | Yearly ATM fee|
| `SMS CHRG` | SMS charge (quaterly)  | Quaterly SMS fee| 
| `CGST/SGST ` | GST charge on sms | GST on SMS |
| `CPM` | contact updation charge | Phone/email |
